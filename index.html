Some Cool Projects I Built!



Yelp Restaurant Survival (2021)


ML Algorithm Developer and Project Reporter (Team Member)

Main ML algorithm developer where I built 5 different models trained on Yelp's public restaurant dataset to extract the most important features that could be used to predict whether or not a restaurant would close.

I then used those results with my team to generate insights for owners to mediate the success and health of their restaurantâ€™s future. For example, I reported the optimal opening and closing times of the more successful restaurants and the pitfalls of restaurants that ended up closing. This would help inform restaurants on the type of next steps they can take to prolong their restuarant's longevity.


GGWP- Identify Toxic Behavior in Gaming (2020)


NLP Algorithm Developer and Insight Reporter (Team Member)

Worked with GGWP startup to identify patterns of toxicity within games by exploring provided 21 million data points derived from Dota 2 chats. My team used NLP for EDA and AWS to store and process the large dataset.

I worked on generating player profiles from my team's results and deployed surveys in some of my Discord gaming communities to determine which forms of toxicity should be given priority by game moderators. The player profiles would help moderators determine what kind of in-game measures they would take next on toxic players(p-bans, timeout, IP blocks, etc).
